INTRODUCTION

5:55 Live in Pokhara is a flagship live music event designed to deliver a high-energy hip-hop concert experience in Nepal’s tourism capital. Featuring one of the country’s most influential contemporary artists, the event brings together music, youth culture, nightlife, and professional production under one stage.

Strategically hosted in Pokhara, the concert aims to attract a diverse audience of local fans, students, travelers, and young professionals. With strong digital presence, live audience engagement, and premium on-ground execution, 5:55 Live in Pokhara offers a powerful platform for brands seeking meaningful visibility and cultural relevance.
